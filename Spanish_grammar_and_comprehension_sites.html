CB

Colby College Spanish

Interactive Award-winning

Comprehensive collection of interactive grammar exercises by Barbara K. Nelson. Includes immediate feedback and excellent cultural content. The "Ojalá que llueva café" module is especially acclaimed for teaching subjunctive.

SS

StudySpanish.com

Comprehensive Audio

Free tutorials with audio, grammar explanations, and verb drills. Clear explanations in English make complex grammar accessible. Perfect for self-study with structured lessons and practice exercises.

LS

Lawless Spanish

CEFR-aligned Detailed

Grammar lessons organized by CEFR levels with in-depth explanations. Excellent B2 upper-intermediate resources. Clear structure makes it easy to find exactly what you need to practice.

L

Lingolia Español

Interactive Examples

Complete grammar section covering all major topics with plenty of examples and interactive exercises. Practice every grammar point in detail with immediate feedback and clear explanations.

SD

SpanishDict

Video Lessons Complete Guide

Expert articles and interactive video lessons on Spanish grammar. Excellent for tricky topics like por vs. para. Includes pronunciation guides, verb conjugation practice, and comprehensive explanations.

LG

Lengalia

Structured Courses Thousands of Exercises

Progressive Spanish courses across six CEFR levels with thousands of exercises and instant results. Dedicated B1 and B2 courses designed for intermediate learners with voice recording features.

SpanishBoat

B1-C1 Printable

Hundreds of free grammar worksheets for B1-C1 levels. Practice online or print as PDF. Perfect for teachers and students who want structured, level-appropriate grammar exercises.

CG

Conjuguemos

Verb Focus Drills & Quizzes

Specialized verb conjugation practice with helpful drills and quizzes. Master all Spanish tenses through repetitive, targeted practice. Essential for building strong conjugation skills at B1-B2.
